IVF Treatment Process

In Vitro Fertilization (IVF) is a medical procedure where eggs and sperm are combined outside the body to create embryos. These embryos are then implanted into the woman‚Äôs uterus for a successful pregnancy. IVF is recommended for couples struggling with infertility, male infertility, or unexplained infertility.

The IVF Procedure Includes:

  • Ovarian stimulation and monitoring of egg production.
  • Egg retrieval and sperm collection.
  • Fertilization and embryo culture in a lab.
  • Embryo transfer to the uterus, followed by pregnancy tests.
  • Continuous support and post-treatment care.

Fertility Preservation: Egg Freezing

Egg freezing allows women to preserve their eggs for future use, ensuring they can start a family at a later stage. This option is suitable for women who want to delay pregnancy for reasons such as career or health concerns.

Benefits of Egg Freezing:

  • Preserve fertility for future pregnancy attempts.
  • Ideal for women with medical conditions affecting fertility.
  • Preserve your eggs until you are ready to start a family.
  • Comprehensive care, from consultation to egg storage.
